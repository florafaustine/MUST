Ongoing Projects

Campus Recycling Initiative

We have placed recycling bins across the campus and are working to educate students about the importance of recycling. Join us every Friday for sorting and processing collected materials.

Progress Update: Over 200 kg of recyclable materials collected in the last month.

Community Garden Project

Our community garden provides fresh produce for students and staff. Volunteers are welcome to help with planting, weeding, and harvesting.

Progress Update: Successfully harvested 50 kg of vegetables this season.

Completed Projects

Beach Clean-Up Drive

In March, we organized a beach clean-up event, where volunteers collected over 300 kg of plastic waste, making our local beach cleaner and safer.

Impact Report: Significant reduction in plastic waste on the beach, improved local marine life health.

Tree Planting Day

We successfully planted over 200 trees around the campus, contributing to a greener and more sustainable environment.

Impact Report: Increased green cover on campus, improved air quality, and enhanced biodiversity.
