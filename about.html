Our Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable and environmentally conscious university community through education, action, and advocacy. We aim to empower students and staff at MUST to engage in practices that protect and improve our natural environment.

Our Objectives

  • Increase awareness about environmental issues among students and staff.
  • Organize activities and projects that directly benefit the environment.
  • Influence university policies to support sustainability and environmental protection.
  • Collaborate with local environmental groups for broader impact.
  • Promote sustainable practices on campus and within the community.

Our History

The Green Guardians of MUST was founded in 2022 by a group of environmentally conscious students who wanted to make a difference. Since then, we have organized numerous successful events, including recycling drives, tree planting days, and sustainability workshops. Our efforts have led to a significant increase in environmental awareness and action within the university community.
